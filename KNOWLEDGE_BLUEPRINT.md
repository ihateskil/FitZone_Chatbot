## 6. Quality Control Framework

### Inclusion Criteria (Must Pass ALL)
1. **Author authority** ? Author holds relevant degree (PhD, MD, RD, DPT, CSCS) OR publication in peer-reviewed journal OR published by recognized professional organization (ACSM, NSCA, ISSN)
2. **Recency** ? Research <10 years old OR is a foundational/landmark study OR is a textbook recognized as standard in field
3. **Reproducibility** ? Claims backed by peer-reviewed research OR expert consensus (position stands) OR established practice with 10+ year track record
4. **Relevance** ? Directly applicable to fitness training, nutrition coaching, or injury prevention for general population

### Exclusion Criteria (Any ONE Fails)
1. **Anecdote-only** ? Claims based solely on personal experience without research backing
2. **Commercial bias** ? Source has financial interest in the claim (supplement company "research", MLM health claims)
3. **Pseudoscience** ? Promotes detox, homeopathy, toning, spot reduction, muscle confusion as scientific concepts
4. **Outdated** ? Pre-2000 exercise science that has been superseded by newer research (unless historical context)
5. **Plagiarized/Duplicated** ? Content copied without attribution from another source
6. **Dangerous advice** ? Recommends techniques with high injury risk without adequate Caveats (e.g., maxing out without warm-up, training through acute pain)

### Quality Scoring System
| Signal | Points | Rationale |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Published in peer-reviewed journal | +3 | Highest evidence quality |
| Systematic review or meta-analysis | +4 | Gold standard of evidence |
| Professional organization position stand | +3 | Expert consensus |
| Author holds relevant PhD/MD | +2 | Qualified expert |
| Cited by >50 other papers | +2 | High impact |
| Published >5 years ago without updates | -1 | Potentially outdated |
| Single study (not replicated) | -1 | Lower confidence |
| Author sells related products | -2 | Potential conflict |
| No methodology section | -3 | Not rigorous |

### Review Process
1. **Automated pre-filter** ? Source metadata check (domain, author, date, citations)
2. **Quality score calculation** ? Apply scoring rubric above
3. **Expert review** (for score < 5) ? Manual assessment by qualified reviewer
4. **Community flagging** ? Users can flag outdated/incorrect entries
5. **Annual audit** ? Review all entries for continued relevance and accuracy
